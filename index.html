About Us

Precision in Every Gauge We Build

Established in 2016 in Aurangabad, Master Profile Gauges is a small-scale industry specializing in the manufacturing of Splined Gauges and Relation Gauges. Our spline gauges ensure accurate measurement of gear and involute-shaped components, while our relation gauges are designed to check positional accuracy for critical specifications like shaft bores and flanges with fastening holes.

With an ISO 9001:2015 certification and recognition from the Marathwada Association of Small Scale Industries & Agriculture (MASSIA) for excellence in manufacturing, we are committed to delivering unmatched quality, precision, and customer satisfaction.
